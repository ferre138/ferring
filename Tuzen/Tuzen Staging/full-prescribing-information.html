Full Prescribing Information

NPN 80019103
Product Code 998

Presentation

Each capsule of Tuzen® contains 10 Billion live lactobacillus plantarum 299v (selected by Probi AB), a probiotic strain that is resistant to gastric acids to ensure optimal activity in the intestines.

Non-medicinal ingredients: Potato starch, magnesium stearate, hypromellose (capsule) lecithin, potassium phosphate monobasic, potassium phosphate dibasic, Vitamin C, sucrose.

 

Recommended Usage

Tuzen helps to reduce flatulence and abdominal pain associated with flatulence in individuals with Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS).

 

Recommended Dosage

Adults: 1 – 2 capsules per day as required with a balanced diet or as recommended by your physician.

 

Caution

Do not use if you experience nausea, fever, vomiting, bloody diarrhea or unusual severe abdominal pain. Discontinue use and consult a healthcare practitioner if symptoms of digestive upset (e.g., diarrhea) occur, worsen or persist beyond 3 days. Not recommended for use in children. Do not take if pregnant, breastfeeding or if immune system is compromised. Contains no artificial preservatives, flavour, colour, salt or gluten (wheat). Has been in contact with soy.

 

Storage

Keep Tuzen at room temperature (15 - 25ºC). Keep out of the reach of children.

 

Supplied

Tuzen is supplied in boxes of 30 capsules.
